## Transaction Fees

All plans include a per-transaction fee on successful contributions:

| Component | Rate |
|---|---|
| Contribution Fee | 1% (max ₦2,000 per transaction) |
| Payout Processing | Free (Kolo fee) |
| Bank Transfer Fees | Nomba's standard rates apply |

### Fee Example

```
Contribution Amount:  ₦50,000
Kolo Fee (1%):        ₦500 (₦50,000 × 0.01)
Max Cap:              ₦2,000
Actual Fee:           ₦500
Group Credit:         ₦49,500
```

## Payment Processing

- **Kolo fees** are deducted automatically during contribution processing
- **Nomba gateway fees** vary by payment method (card, bank transfer, USSD)
- All fees are transparently displayed before any payment is confirmed
- No hidden charges

## Refund Policy

- Failed transactions are fully refunded
- Kolo's platform fee is waived on failed/refunded transactions
- Subscription fees are non-refundable but can be prorated for upgrades
