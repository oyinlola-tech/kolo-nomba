## 1. Introduction

Kolo is a digital cooperative savings and payment infrastructure platform designed to modernize traditional African savings systems including Ajo, Esusu, thrift contributions, and cooperative financial management.

This software contains proprietary technology, business logic, architecture, integrations, payment workflows, and implementation details owned by Oluwayemi Oyinlola.
